Who I am

Thanks for visiting my blog!

Hello, my name is HanziZhu(朱汉子), a programmer who enjoys finding simple solutions to complex problems.

I hold a Master’s degree in Mechanical Design from Hefei University of Technology, where my research focused on robot dynamics and analytical modeling.

In my professional work, I specialize in backend engineering, service-oriented architecture, and DevOps practices.

I have hands-on experience designing and implementing scalable services, particularly within containerized environments using K8s & Docker. My technical background includes proficiency in Python, Golang, and C, and I am currently advancing my expertise in Rust.

I am also experienced in building and maintaining systems based on MySQL and PostgreSQL, with a focus on performance, reliability, and operational efficiency.

In addition, I have practical experience with cloud computing, and I am familiar with a wide range of AWS services, including compute, storage, networking, and deployment tools commonly used in modern infrastructure workflows.

I am committed to developing robust technical solutions and continuously improving my engineering capabilities.
